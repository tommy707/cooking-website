Hot chocolate recipe

Do YOU love hot chocolate?!

Well, I loooove hot chocolate! I drink it on a Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday and Sunday! But NEVER on a Wednesday.

There is only one thing I don't really like about hot chocolate. You guessed it- it is so darn hard to make. When I get back from a grueling 3 hour workday, all I want to do is sit down and rest my poor, tired feet. I simply don't have the energy to follow other websites recipes of hot chocolate. So I decided to make my OWN recipe. Something that is simple, quick, and delicious. My name is Bartholomew and I'm glad to have you here!

Ingredients

  • hot chocolate powder
  • 1/3 cup of water
  • 3 marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup of finely chopped dragon egg shell
  • a block of pure gold
  • dried unicorn droppings
  • Half-life 3

Method

  1. Add all ingredients to cup
  2. Run a marathon and come 1st
  3. Do seventy three pushups before drinking for maximum taste
  4. DO NOT drink on Wednesday.
  5. Enjoy!
