Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 5 lemons
  • 2 eggs
  • 7 ounces of children tears
  • baking powder
  • 5 cups of sugar
  • Hawaiian citizenship

Icing

  • 3 bags icing sugar
  • bucket of water

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients.
  2. Crack an egg in your mouth to ensure it is still fresh (It will taste nice). Then, crack remaining into bowl.
  3. Peel lemons and squeeze the juice into mixture. Don't lick your fingers as tempting as it is.
  4. Okay, you can lick your fingers a little.
  5. Panfry children tears before adding them to the mixture. They contain harmful bacteria if not cooked properly.
  6. Bake in oven for three hours at 700c.
  7. Combine icing ingredients and dollop on top.

Eat hot and enjoy. This will last up to a year in the fridge if you do not mind the mold... But it never lasts that long in my house. LOL!
